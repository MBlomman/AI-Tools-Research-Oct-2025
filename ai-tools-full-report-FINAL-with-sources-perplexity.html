⚖️ Legal & Compliance Guide for Swedish Agencies

🇪🇺 EU AI Act Compliance Timeline

⏰ CRITICAL DEADLINE: August 2, 2026
Mandatory machine-readable watermarking for ALL AI-generated content. Penalty for non-compliance: €15M or 3% of global annual turnover, whichever is higher.

What You Must Do NOW

  1. Implement Watermarking: Use tools with built-in watermarking (Google SynthID, Adobe Content Credentials)
  2. Document AI Usage: Track all AI tools used, what content was generated, when, and by whom
  3. GDPR Compliance: Ensure all tools are GDPR-compliant (all recommended tools are)
  4. Client Contracts: Update contracts to specify AI usage and watermarking
  5. Staff Training: Train all staff on compliance requirements

Risk Assessment Matrix

Risk Level Tools Issues Recommendation
LOW RISK Google (Imagen, Veo, Gemini), Adobe Firefly, Claude, Perplexity, HeyGen, ElevenLabs, Soundraw None - full compliance ✅ USE FOR ALL WORK
MODERATE RISK ChatGPT, DALL-E 3, Runway, Luma, Pika, Ideogram GDPR compliant but limited transparency on training data ✅ OK for client work - Document usage
HIGH RISK Midjourney Active litigation (Disney, Warner Bros, Universal) ⚠️ AVOID for client deliverables
VERY HIGH RISK Suno, Udio, Kling, Hailuo, Wan Active lawsuits OR no GDPR compliance 🚨 DO NOT USE commercially

GDPR Compliance Checklist

  • ✅ Data Residency: All recommended tools offer EU data centers
  • ✅ Data Processing Agreements: Available from all major vendors
  • ✅ No Training on User Data: Enterprise tiers guarantee this (Claude Business, ChatGPT Enterprise, Google Workspace)
  • ✅ Right to Deletion: All recommended tools support GDPR deletion requests
  • ✅ Transparency: Tools disclose AI usage in generated content
